## What ProofKeep Receipts Does

ProofKeep Receipts helps users scan, classify, organize, search, and save purchase-related proof such as receipts, return labels, shipping proof, warranty information, grocery receipts, and purchase screenshots.

The current app is local-only. It does not require an account, does not use a backend, and does not upload photos or OCR text to a server.
