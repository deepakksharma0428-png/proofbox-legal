# ProofBox Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ProofBox handles information.

## Summary

The current ProofBox prototype is local-only:

- No account is required.
- No backend is used.
- No photos are uploaded.
- No OCR text is uploaded.
- No analytics are included.
- No ads are included.
- No tracking is included.
- No personal data is sold.

## Who Operates ProofBox

ProofBox is owned and operated by Deepak Sharma. Copyright (c) 2026 Deepak Sharma. All rights reserved.

## Information Processed On Your Device

ProofBox may process the following information locally on your device:

- Photos and screenshots you select or allow the app to scan.
- OCR text extracted from images.
- Receipt details such as store names, dates, totals, taxes, and order numbers when detected.
- Return label or shipping details such as tracking numbers, carrier names, QR/barcode payloads, or return-related text when detected.
- Warranty details such as product names, serial/model numbers, warranty dates, and coverage text when detected.
- Notes, titles, due dates, classifications, and edits you manually enter.

This information is used to classify, name, organize, search, and remind you about saved proof items.

## Photo Library Access

ProofBox asks for photo library access so it can scan screenshots and photos for purchase-related proof. You may grant full access, limited access, or deny access through iOS settings.

When scanning, ProofBox uses on-device processing. Matching items are copied into ProofBox's local app storage so they can be searched and organized inside the app.

## Camera Access

ProofBox asks for camera access so you can scan receipts, labels, warranties, and shipping proof directly.

## Notifications

ProofBox may ask for notification permission so it can remind you before return windows, warranty dates, or other due dates you save in the app.

## Data Storage

ProofBox stores app data locally on your device, including saved proof records, local image copies, OCR text, notes, classifications, and due dates.

The current prototype does not sync data to a server. If you delete the app, local app data may be deleted according to iOS behavior.

## Data Sharing

The current prototype does not share your saved proof data with Deepak Sharma, analytics providers, advertisers, data brokers, or third-party cloud services.

If you manually share, export, message, email, or otherwise send proof items outside ProofBox, that sharing is controlled by you and may be handled by Apple's share system or other apps you choose.

## Tracking and Advertising

ProofBox does not track you across apps or websites and does not include advertising in the current prototype.

## Children's Privacy

ProofBox is not intended for children under 13. The app is designed as a productivity tool for organizing purchase proof.

## Security

ProofBox uses local device storage and Apple's platform frameworks. No method of storage is perfectly secure. You are responsible for protecting your device, passcode, Apple ID, backups, and physical access to your phone.

## Your Choices

You can:

- Deny or limit photo access in iOS Settings.
- Deny camera access in iOS Settings.
- Deny notification access in iOS Settings.
- Delete saved proof items inside the app.
- Delete the app to remove local app data, subject to iOS behavior and backups.

## Future Changes

If future versions add accounts, cloud sync, analytics, subscriptions, AI services, or third-party integrations, this Privacy Policy and App Store privacy disclosures should be updated before release.

## Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact method listed in the App Store listing or official ProofBox support page once published.
