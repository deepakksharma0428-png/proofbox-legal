ProofBox Legal

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 9, 2026

Summary

The current ProofBox app is local-only. No account is required. No backend is used. No photos are uploaded. No OCR text is uploaded. No analytics, ads, or tracking are included. No personal data is sold.

Who Operates ProofBox

ProofBox is owned and operated by Deepak Sharma.

Information Processed On Your Device

ProofBox may process photos and screenshots you select or allow the app to scan, OCR text extracted from images, receipt details, return label or shipping details, warranty details, notes, titles, due dates, classifications, and edits you manually enter.

Photo Library Access

ProofBox asks for photo library access so it can scan screenshots and photos for purchase-related proof. You may grant full access, limited access, or deny access through iOS settings. When scanning, ProofBox uses on-device processing.

Camera Access

ProofBox asks for camera access so you can scan receipts, labels, warranties, and shipping proof directly.

Notifications

ProofBox may ask for notification permission so it can remind you before return windows, warranty dates, or other due dates you save in the app.

Data Storage

ProofBox stores app data locally on your device, including saved proof records, local image copies, OCR text, notes, classifications, and due dates. The current app does not sync data to a server.

Data Sharing

The current app does not share your saved proof data with Deepak Sharma, analytics providers, advertisers, data brokers, or third-party cloud services. If you manually share or export proof items outside ProofBox, that sharing is controlled by you.

Tracking and Advertising

ProofBox does not track you across apps or websites and does not include advertising in the current app.

Children's Privacy

ProofBox is not intended for children under 13. The app is designed as a productivity tool for organizing purchase proof.

Your Choices

You can deny or limit photo access, camera access, and notification access in iOS Settings. You can delete saved proof items inside the app. You can delete the app to remove local app data, subject to iOS behavior and backups.

Future Changes

If future versions add accounts, cloud sync, analytics, subscriptions, AI services, or third-party integrations, this Privacy Policy and App Store privacy disclosures should be updated before release.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact method listed in the App Store listing or official ProofBox support page once published.
