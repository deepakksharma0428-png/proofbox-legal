ProofKeep Receipts Support

For ProofKeep Receipts support, privacy questions, legal questions, App Store review questions, bug reports, general feedback, or feature requests, email proofkeepreceipts@gmail.com.

If you are reviewing this app before publication, use the contact information provided in App Store Connect.

Before Requesting Support

ProofKeep Receipts is local-first. If photo access, camera access, or notifications do not work, check iOS Settings for ProofKeep Receipts permissions. If a scan misses an item, the item can be added manually using the app's camera or photo import flow.

Privacy Reminder

The current ProofKeep Receipts app does not use a backend, account system, analytics, advertising, tracking, or cloud sync. Saved proof records are stored locally on the user's device.
