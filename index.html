ProofKeep Receipts

Legal and Privacy

ProofKeep Receipts, also referred to as ProofKeep Receipts in these pages, is a local-first app for finding receipts, return labels, shipping proof, warranties, and purchase screenshots. The current app does not use a backend, analytics, ads, tracking, or account system.

Privacy Position

ProofKeep Receipts processes photos, screenshots, OCR text, labels, reminders, and saved proof records locally on the user's device in the current app. The current app does not upload proof data to a server.

Who ProofKeep Receipts Is For

ProofKeep Receipts is for adults and everyday users who want a simple way to find and organize purchase-related proof they already saved, including receipts, return labels, warranty screenshots, shipping proof, grocery receipts, and purchase screenshots.

Who ProofKeep Receipts Is Not For

ProofKeep Receipts is not a legal, financial, tax, warranty, insurance, shipping, refund, return-policy, chargeback, or consumer-rights advisory service. Users must verify deadlines, extracted text, return rules, warranty coverage, claim rules, and other important details independently.

Paid Features

ProofKeep Receipts may offer an optional one-time ProofKeep Receipts Pro lifetime unlock through Apple In-App Purchase. Free users may save up to 50 lifetime proof items. Deleting saved items does not reset the lifetime free-save count. Pro users may unlock unlimited saved proof, subject to app functionality and App Store rules.
