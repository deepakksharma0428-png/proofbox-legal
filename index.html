ProofBox

Legal and Privacy

ProofBox is a local-first app for finding receipts, return labels, shipping proof, warranties, and purchase screenshots. The current app does not use a backend, analytics, ads, tracking, or account system.

Privacy Position

ProofBox processes photos, screenshots, OCR text, labels, reminders, and saved proof records locally on the user's device in the current app. The current app does not upload proof data to a server.

Who ProofBox Is For

ProofBox is for adults and everyday users who want a simple way to find and organize purchase-related proof they already saved, including receipts, return labels, warranty screenshots, shipping proof, grocery receipts, and purchase screenshots.

Who ProofBox Is Not For

ProofBox is not a legal, financial, tax, warranty, insurance, shipping, refund, return-policy, chargeback, or consumer-rights advisory service. Users must verify deadlines, extracted text, return rules, warranty coverage, claim rules, and other important details independently.
